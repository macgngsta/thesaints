FAQ

Have a question? We have answers!

If you still have questions, feel free to give us a call or fill out the contact form.

Contact Us
Who provides the materials?

You have the choice to purchase, pick up, and deliver the materials yourself or we will be happy to do these things for you.

What kind of payments do you accept?

We accept cash, personal checks, cashier's checks, and some locations accept credit & debit cards as well as Paypal.

What are the payment terms?

For handyman services payment is expected at the end of the job. For jobs requiring a contract it will be outlined that an initial deposit of 50% of the overall job bid is required. This covers the appointment time slot, project timeframe, permits if necessary as well as material costs.

What are your service areas?

We currently serve all of King County. The Saint has reliable transporation and a truck to assist with material pick up if necessary.

What is the process like?

The process is as follows:

  1. 1. Make an appointment for a quote.
  2. 2. The Saint Handy Man Services will show up at our appointed time, evaluate the work that needs to be done.
  3. 3. We will ask a series of questions in case we're not sure about anything. During this time you can also ask us questions as well and let us know what you are looking for/what your expectations are.
  4. 4. We will send you a quote and if you choose to accept we will email you a formal bid including the scope of work details. For handy man services a contract is not necessary, however, for projects of medium to larger scales in which the bid is over $1,000.00 a contract will be required.
  5. 5. Once the quote is approved, and contract signed (if applicable), we will schedule a timeframe and dates to begin the work.
