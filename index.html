James has always found a passion in working with his hands through his experience in construction during his early years helping his father.

James has a passion for helping people and making their homes functional and inviting spaces that they enjoy.

Beth is his wife and helps with the organizational aspect and scheduling aspect of the business, while James is the one with all hands on deck.

Together they are the team at, The Saint Handyman Services.
