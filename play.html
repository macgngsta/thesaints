Contact Us
Who provides the materials?
You have the choice to purchase, pick up, and deliver the materials yourself or we will be happy to do these things for you. To help you make an informed decision, we can provide information on all the current building materials & products available on the market, including colors, shapes, sizes, durability, and warranty of these products.
Do you offer financing?
Final Payment is due upon completion and your successful inspection of our work. If you need help in financing your project we can guide you to resources which specialize in home improvement financing for homeowners. We accept cash, personal checks, cashier's checks, and some locations accept credit & debit cards as well.
How do you charge for services?
We provide a free fixed-cost quote so that you are always in charge of your home maintenance and improvement projects. There are many ways of accomplishing certain tasks and we will share with you the most cost-effective processes available to meet your budget. The quoted price will never change unless the scope of work changes.
What is the process like?
The process is as follows:
1. Make an appointment for a quote if you are looking to have contract us for a medium to large scale project.
2. The Saint Handy Man Services will show up at our appointed time, evaluate the work that needs to be done.
We will ask a series of questions in case we're not sure about anything. During this time you can also ask us questions as well and let us know what you are looking for/what your expectations are.
We will send you a quote and if you choose to accept we will email you a formal bid including the scope of work details.
Once the quote is approved, and contract signed, we will schedule a timeframe and dates to begin the work.
